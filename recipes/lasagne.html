Lasagne Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

    For the meat sauce

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar(optional)
  • Salt

    To assemble the lasagna

  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1/4 pound freshly grated Parmesan cheese
